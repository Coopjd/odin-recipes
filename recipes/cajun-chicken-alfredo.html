Cajun Chicken Alfredo

Description: A delicious, easy, and filling dish of cajun chicken alfredo that feeds the whole family!

    Ingredients:
  • Boneless skinless chicken breast
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Unsalted butter
  • Fresh garlic
  • Chicken broth or chicken stock
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Parmesan cheese - Freshly grated
  • Pasta - Fettuccine or linguine
  • Pasta water
  • Parsley - Optional, for garnish
    Steps:
  1. Dry chicken breasts off with paper towels
  2. Mince garlic
  3. Grate parmesan cheese
  4. Preheat a large skillet over medium heat
  5. Cook the pasta for just a few minutes under the package instructions until it's al dente (be sure to save 1 cup of pasta water to thin the sauce)
  6. Measure out all remaining ingredients
  7. Season the chicken liberally with cajun seasoning blend and kosher salt
  8. Add 2 Tablespoons of butter to hot skillet. Add Chicken and sear on all sides over medium-high heat, until internal temperature is 165 degrees F
  9. Remove chicken and slice into pieces. Don't drain any drippings from the skillet
  10. Reduce the heat and add remaining butter. Add splash of chicken broth and minced garlic to skillet.
  11. Cook until flagrant
  12. Reduce to low heat. Add heavy cream
  13. Stir in parmesan cheese slowly, adding in small handfuls, stirring to melt before adding the next handful
  14. Season with the remaining cajun seasoning, and salt to taste
  15. Add the cooked pasta, and a little bit of pasta water, along with parsley.
  16. Toss to combine, adding more pasta water until the sauce clings to the noodles.
  17. Add the chicken pieces back over top, sprinkle with more parsley and freshly grated parmesan.
