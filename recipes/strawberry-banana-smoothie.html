Strawberry-Banana Smoothie

Description: A delicious and easy to make strawberry-banana smoothie

    Ingredients:
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • Pinch of ground cinnamon
    Steps:
  1. Blend the banana, strawberries, yogurt, milk, honey, cinnamon, and 1 cup of ice in a blender until smooth
  2. Pour into a glass
